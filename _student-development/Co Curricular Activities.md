<p>CCA is considered a core but non-examinable module at NUS High School.
It is compulsory for every student to participate in one school-based CCA
from any of the 4 CCA categories: Clubs &amp; Societies, Performing Arts,
Sports and Uniformed Groups.</p>
<p>Click&nbsp;<strong><a href="/files/CCA/ccabrochure.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a></strong>&nbsp;to
take a look at our CCA e-Brochure for 2024, prepared for our Virtual CCA
Fair!&nbsp;<em>(file takes a while to download)</em>
</p>
