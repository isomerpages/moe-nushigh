<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>All registrations must be submitted online; telephone registrations will
not be accepted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to obtain registration information directly from their respective
schools. Schools have the discretion to determine the number of participants
and select students for participation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only MOE primary schools are allowed to register.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Registration fee: <strong>$25 nett</strong> (inclusive of 9% GST) per participant.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The period for registration is from <strong>20 February to 20 March 2026</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
